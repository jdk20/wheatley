1. Open the directory: C:\Users\SiapasLab\Desktop\git\wheatley
2. Open the VI: dsm-main.vi
3. Enter the "Animal Name" on the front panel.
4. Select the correct "Protocol" on the front panel.
	a. Partial Training 1: All tones match, reward always dispensed, run this for 2 days.
	b. Partial Training 2: All tones match, reward dispensed when licked, run this until animal >80% accuracy.
	c. Full Training: Both match and non-match tones, reward dispensed when licked, run this until animal >80% accuracy.
5. Enter the estimated "Reward Volume".
6. Press the Run arrow. 
7. If the VI needs to be stopped, use the Stop button on the front panel.
